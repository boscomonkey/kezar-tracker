Kezar Tracker Web App

GPS running app that displays instantaneous speed; plus lap time, distance, and average speed. Designed for the style of running workouts practiced by the San Francisco Triathlon Club (at Kezar Track, Golden Gate Park) composed of non-stop laps of different length, speed, and duration.

Currently available mobile apps such as Strava and Runkeeper suffer from some major problems for SF Tri style of track practice:

  1. They average out speed over the duration of the whole workout. Whereas we need instantaneous speed.
  2. They do not offer a lap timer. Whereas we need to run very specific lap durations.

Thus, I often have to use both a running app and a stopwatch app to follow my workout. The goal of this Kezar Tracker Web App is to provide a super simple running tracker for the SF Tri style of track workouts.
